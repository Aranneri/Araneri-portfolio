Curiosity as a compass,

As an electronics engineer, I am driven by a deep curiosity to explore real-world challenges and develop impactful digital solutions. Space science and physics are the ultimate anchors that endlessly captivate my imagination and inspire my technical approach.

I thrive in collaborative environments where deep technical work is balanced with spontaneous, visionary conversations about the cosmos. With a structured mindset, I ensure that every project I undertake is strategically planned before it is meticulously executed.

In moments of creative chaos or tight deadlines, I bring a steady, grounding calmness that helps everyone step back, breathe, and navigate roadblocks with clarity — keeping the team moving forward like a well-synchronized orbit.
