About

Curiosity
fuels the craft.

I am an undergraduate engineering student — someone who lives at the intersection of technical precision and creative expression. My days are shaped by circuits and signals, but my mind wanders freely into worlds built of words.

I believe that the best work happens when discipline meets imagination. Whether I am decoding a waveform or crafting a sentence, I bring the same measured intentionality to every task.

Approach & Style

I am most powerful when I work alone — with silence, focus, and my own rhythm. Solo work is where my ideas breathe freely and my standards run highest.


But I do not shy away from collaboration. When the task calls for it, I adapt. I listen, contribute, and move with the team — never losing sight of the goal.


Versatile enough to work on anything I am able to do — and disciplined enough to do it well.
