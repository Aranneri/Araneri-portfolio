ABOUT ME

I'm Kishore, a passionate video editor who lives for the moment a timeline comes together — when music, motion, and colour align to create something that makes you feel.

My editing style is built on emotion, energy, and clean visual identity. Every cut has intention. Every colour grade has mood. Every transition has a story.

I work on promotional videos, social media edits, cinematic posters, intro videos, and creative content — always pushing to improve craft and workflow to deliver professional-quality output.

I'm constantly learning new editing techniques, exploring new motion design patterns, and refining my visual storytelling approach.
