About Me

Where design meets
engineering roots

I am a Designer and Frontend Developer with a background in Electronics and Instrumentation Engineering. I enjoy the intersection of technical thinking and creative expression — building things that look good and work well.

I'm drawn to clean, purposeful design that communicates clearly. Whether it's a visual, a circuit simulation, or a web interface, I bring a detail-oriented and thoughtful approach to everything I work on.

I enjoy working on projects that challenge me to think both creatively and analytically — from designing interfaces to building embedded systems and developing full simulation environments.

Being part of Araneri gives me the space to explore, create, and grow alongside a team of passionate people from different domains. I love how Araneri brings together individuality and collaboration in a single creative space.
