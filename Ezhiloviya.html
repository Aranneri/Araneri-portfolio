01 / About

Dreaming beyond
ordinary limits.

I'm someone who constantly dreams beyond ordinary limits. My interests revolve around technology, storytelling, space exploration, and building meaningful ideas that leave a lasting impact.

I enjoy working on projects that combine creativity with logic. I love mathematics, logical thinking, space science, physics — everything that involves breaking the previous patterns of human belief and bringing new ways of thinking about a problem in an unconventional way.

My approach toward work is intense, detail-oriented, and vision-driven. I believe every project should have purpose, emotion, and identity — instead of just existing for the sake of completion. I always look into the reason for existence before stepping into anything.

I enjoy experimenting with bold ideas and turning chaotic thoughts into structured creations. I believe everything has a purpose except for human beings, who are gifted enough to choose their own — and I will make sure I nurture that gift and build a life driven by vision, values, and voice.

And yes — I am proudly a Potterhead. Currently working on writing my own psychological thriller. I can work all 24 hours if I love something too much — and the opposite is equally true.
