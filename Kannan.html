About Me

I am fundamentally a writer — someone who finds meaning in crafting Tamil stories that speak to real lives, real struggles, and real growth. Words, for me, are not just tools; they are conversations waiting to happen.

My interests span Tamil literature, entrepreneurship, and business thinking — a mix that pushes me to write with both heart and strategy. I am always learning, always looking for the next idea that can change how I see the world.

Beyond writing, I bring the same curiosity to designing, editing, and teaching. I believe knowledge shared simply is the most powerful kind of knowledge.

My personality: straightforward, polite, and honest — with kindness at the core.
